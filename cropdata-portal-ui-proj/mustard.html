Mustard

Mustard is the third-most important oilseed crop after soybean and groundnut, contributing 20-25% of total oilseed production in the country. Mustard is mainly grown in North-Western parts of India. Major mustard-producing states are Rajasthan, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Gujarat, West Bengal, and Assam.
