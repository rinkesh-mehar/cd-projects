Maize

Globally, maize is known as the queen of cereals because of its highest genetic yield potential among cereals. In India, maize is the third-most important food crop after rice and wheat. Maize is cultivated throughout the year in all states of the country. Major maize-growing states include Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh. Nepal, Bangladesh, Vietnam, Pakistan and the Philippines are the export markets for maize.
