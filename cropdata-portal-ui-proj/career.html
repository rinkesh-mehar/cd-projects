Why CropData?

We provide an opportunity to transform the lives of the poorest, most deprived sections of our society. More income, with greater predictability, has a cascading effect on the rural poor. Increased income leads to better nutrition, better health, better education, greater employability, greater consumption, and spending, improving the overall rural economy. Our ecosystem provides easy access to credit, government subsidies, and services, essential for a dignified life. This journey that we have embarked on, cannot be achieved without a passionate, compassionate, and an ethical team, with an optimistic view of the world, brave enough to accept the challenge to solve chronic and complex problems that have lingered on for generations.
