Dry Chili

Dry Chili is one of the most important commercial spice crops worldwide. India is one of the largest producers and consumers of chili in the world. Indian chilies are known for their pungency and color. Andhra Pradesh is the major chili-producing state in India, followed by Maharashtra, Karnataka, Orissa, and Madhya Pradesh. Major export markets include Malaysia, Sri Lanka, Pakistan, Indonesia, the United States (USA), Bangladesh, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), China, Thailand, Mexico, United Kingdom, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, and Germany.
