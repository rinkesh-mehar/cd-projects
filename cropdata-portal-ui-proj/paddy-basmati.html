Paddy Basmati

Basmati is known for its special fragrance and flavour in the world. In India, it occupies a special status in rice cultivation because of its high demand in National & International markets. India accounts for over 70% of the world's basmati rice production. The major growing states are Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Delhi, Uttarakhand, Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh. The major export market for basmati rice is Iran, Saudi Arabia, Iraq, UAE, Kuwait, Yemen Republic, USA, and the UK.
